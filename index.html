Brand

Definition + direction, shown in components below.

Brand definition

What’s the company about?

Colour Quest Studio is a startup indie game and narrative studio based around multi-media narratives for a grand story set in multiple games and stories. This guide preps for a landing page to pre-order upcoming games and e-novels.

Who is the company (as a person)?

A small team or a single artist, programmer, and storyteller with an established fanbase and following from existing stories. (Think of popular devs like Toby Fox or Scott Cawthon)

Why might someone buy?

People who like mystery, lore, and worldbuilding can follow ongoing stories, support releases, and pre-order games. The studio offers consistent tone, memorable characters, and an alternate-reality game-like presentation for community mystery solving.

Brand direction

Tone: archival / quiet / almost secretive
Layout: spacious, content-first, high contrast black and white
Interaction: obvious on hover/focus, never loud
