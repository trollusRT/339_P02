Blacksite Web Story

A transcript-style web novel with optional hidden annotations for curious readers.

Type: Web novel Release: Ongoing

Gargoyle, designated Agent 14 is a crafty, mute operative, designated by her superior to increase rapport between her top-secret magical weapons facility and a foreign government. Her operation is thrown to the wayside when her superior and The Blacksite itself are exposed and dismantled. Her quest to rescue her arrested boss has her resort to all sorts of foul play, and even finding out secrets of the land that are so much bigger than any other party.
