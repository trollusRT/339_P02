About the Studio

Colour Quest Studio

A small narrative-first indie studio focused on mystery-driven games, web stories, and long-form interconnected lore.

Overview

What the studio is and why it exists.

Colour Quest Studio is built around long-form storytelling across games, web experiences, and experimental digital formats. Each release contributes to a shared universe where themes of colour, perception, artificial realism, and hidden entities unfold slowly over time.

The goal is not just to release standalone projects, but to create an archive — something players can return to, analyze, and discuss. Mystery is intentional. Silence is intentional. Every detail exists for a reason.

The Creators

The person behind the work.

Independent Developers

Colour Quest Studio currently operates as a team of small independent developers. Projects are designed, illustrated, and structured in-house, tailored for immersion!

Inspiration comes from internet-era storytelling, ARG design, retro web aesthetics, and narrative-heavy indie games.

Creative Philosophy

How projects are approached.

  • Story comes before mechanics.
  • Atmosphere matters more than spectacle.
  • Minimal UI keeps focus on narrative.
  • Community interpretation is part of the design.
  • Silence and ambiguity are tools — not mistakes.
